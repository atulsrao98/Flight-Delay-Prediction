# Flight Delay Prediction
Flight Delays are a major concern in mordern air travel. It depends on a variety of factors and one small delay can affect a multitude of other flights. Having a robust model to determine in order to determine if a given flight will be delayed or not becomes a necessitiy. This notebook provides certain insights into the data as well as one of the many algorithms that can be utilized to tackle this hurdle.

## GETTING STARTED
The dataset utilized is the 2015 Flights Delays Dataset provided by the United States Department of Transportation.
